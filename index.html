SC Labs

About

SC Labs is a shared space for anyone at SoundCloud — Designers, Engineers, Product Managers, or anyone else — to publish and share prototypes they've created. Whether it's an early concept, a polished flow, or a quick experiment, this is the place to put it in front of the team.

What belongs here
Early concepts
Rough ideas, explorations, or lo-fi flows you want feedback on before investing more time.
Interactive prototypes
Figma links, coded prototypes, or hosted demos that show a feature in motion.
Works in progress
Things still being built. Use the "In Progress" status so others know it's evolving.
How to get started
01
Host your prototype
Your prototype needs a public URL. Common options include Vercel, Netlify, GitHub Pages, or a Figma share link. If it's already live somewhere, you're good to go.
02
Click "Submit" in the top navigation
Hit the orange Submit button in the top-right corner of any page. A form will appear asking for your project title, URL, category, status, and a short description.
03
Fill in the details
Add a clear title, paste your URL, pick a category that best fits your team (Design, Engineering, Product, etc.), and write a one-liner about what the prototype is exploring. Tags are optional but help with filtering.
04
Submit and share
Once submitted, your project will appear in the directory immediately. Share the link with your team or drop it in a relevant Slack channel to get feedback.
05
Keep it updated
Use the edit button inside the viewer to update your status as the project moves from "In Progress" to "In Review" or "Shipped". This keeps the directory accurate for everyone.
Projects are stored locally in your browser. For now, this is an internal tool — keep links and descriptions appropriate for a team audience.
